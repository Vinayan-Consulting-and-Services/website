    Breath Alcohol Analyzer

    Device to detect alcohol level

    Vinayan's Breath Alcohol Analyzer, also known as a Breathalyzer, is a device used to measure the concentration of alcohol in a person's breath. It is commonly employed by law enforcement agencies, workplace safety programs, and in various alcohol-related legal or regulatory contexts. The device works on the principle that the amount of alcohol in a person's breath is proportional to the amount of alcohol in their blood (blood alcohol concentration or BAC), which is an indicator of impairment due to alcohol consumption.

    Laser Speed Device

    Device to monitor the speed of vehicles passing.

    Vinayan's laser speed device, often referred to as a laser speed gun or LIDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) speed gun, is a handheld or vehicle-mounted device used by law enforcement officers to measure the speed of vehicles on the road accurately. These devices use laser technology to determine the speed of a vehicle by calculating the time it takes for a laser beam to travel to the vehicle, reflect back, and return to the device.

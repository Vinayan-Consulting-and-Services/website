Body Warm Camera for heat detection

Device to detect body temperature

Vinayan's body-warm camera appears to refer to a thermal imaging camera or infrared camera that is designed to detect and display the heat signatures or thermal radiation emitted by warm objects, including the human body. Thermal cameras are used in search and rescue operations to locate individuals, especially in low-light conditions, through smoke, or in situations where traditional cameras may be less effective.
